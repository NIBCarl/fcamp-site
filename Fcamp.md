What is FCAMP?FCAMP stands for Friendship Camping. It is a specialized youth ministry camp organized by the Seventh Day Adventist Church. Within the Southwestern Philippine Union Conference and specifically NEMM, it serves as a major evangelistic and moral recovery program. The camp is designed primarily for Adventist youth aged 13 to 18 to invite and connect with their non Adventist friends in a warm and engaging environment.Core ObjectivesThe primary goal of Friendship Camping is evangelism through relationship building. The specific objectives include:Fostering spiritual renewalCultivating meaningful friendshipsAdvancing personal growthMentoring future youth leadersPromoting the teachings of Jesus in daily lifeThe FCAMP Timeline and PhasesThe program operates on a comprehensive schedule rather than just a one time event. It is divided into four distinct phases:Phase 1 Spirituality: A whole year round focus on spiritual preparation and readiness.Phase 2 Specialization: Usually held in April. This phase involves mission wide training for camp directors, deputies, and scribes. The training emphasizes leadership, mental health, and spiritual development.Phase 3 Camp Proper: The actual one week camping event which traditionally takes place in July.Phase 4 Sustainability: Post camp nurturing and follow up that continues all year round to keep the youth engaged.Organizational Structure and Core TeamsExecuting an FCAMP requires massive coordination. The structural framework utilized by NEMM involves 12 core teams to keep the camp running smoothly:AdministratorsGuide Team (Coordinators)Camp DirectorPlus One and ScribeBuilder TeamPatrol TeamMedical and Sanitation TeamRanger TeamFriendship TeamYummy Team (In charge of meals)Explorer TeamDocumentation and Media TeamCamp ActivitiesThe camp features various physical, social, and spiritual activities designed to break the ice and build trust among teenagers. Some of the interactive activities include:Trust FallsKnot TyingThree Truths and a LieHot Seat and Fire Feelings CubeGroup discussions on health, relating to parents, and studying spiritual doctrines like the SabbathFCAMP in the Northeastern Mindanao Mission (NEMM)NEMM covers territories in the Caraga region including Agusan del Norte, Dinagat Islands, and Surigao del Norte. The mission headquarters is located in Baan, Butuan City.Under the current leadership of the Youth Ministries Director Pastor Anecito C. Pableo, NEMM has seen incredible success with this youth program. During the camp season in July 2024, the Southwestern region hosted thousands of campers. NEMM recorded the largest turnout in the entire region, successfully bringing in 1,684 non Adventist participants. The event provided a safe space for young people to discover the Adventist faith, resulting in thousands of life changing decisions and baptisms across Mindanao.
